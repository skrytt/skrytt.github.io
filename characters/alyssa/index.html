Alyssa

An artist who has trouble remembering her past.

Alyssa [She/Her] is played by Desislava.

Forgotten Past

One day, Alyssa woke up in an alley and couldn't remember anything: no memory of friends, relatives or even herself... only the vague feeling that she was meant to be running away from something. She hopes to remember who she is and where she is from.

Self-Critical Designer

Alyssa works hard to design useful equipment and often loses herself in her work. However, she often feels her work is not good enough to show to others.

Broken Camera

Alyssa carries a broken SLR camera with a severely damaged film. She knows it must be important for some reason, though isn't sure why.
