Santi

A postgraduate Biologist who will study Feydeep's fauna.

Santi [He/Him] is played by Ryan.

Researcher of Fauna and Flora

Santi's academic passions have seen him graduate from study at the Garden, a renowned University facility advancing the field of biological study.

Postgraduate Funding

The University department has funded Santi's postgraduate research project to study the fluorescent mycenia of Feydeep, and hopefully bring back some to Garden for further study.

Eccentric Chef

Never shy to take a culinary risk, Santi is known for cooking up eccentric and experimental dishes! What new meal could be cooked up from those bats in the tunnels of Feydeep?
