Nefer-ra

A mystical High Priest who collects rare texts and artwork.

Nefer-ra [He/Him] is played by Taneash.

Temple Keeper

Nefer-ra does what he can to preserve his religious Temple's future and avert God's wrath, which he does by trading the Temple's rare materials and by securing new manuscripts and artwork. He dreams of establishing a healthy trade centre at the Temple.

The Book Of Going Forth By Day

Some think magic is rare. Some don't even believe in it! But the wise Nefer-ra's collection includes this rare tome of practical spells to help navigate his problems.

Artisan Collector

Nefer-ra owns a vast array of books and artwork. He's interested in new and rare artifacts of both kinds. Rumours of a hidden and powerful book within Feydeep have captured his fascination.
