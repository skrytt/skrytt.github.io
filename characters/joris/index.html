Joris

A disenfranchised medic seeking a fresh start.

Joris Finch [He/Him] is played by Andy.

Medical Expert

Joris is a hard-working surgeon, top of his class. He is an experienced surgical practitioner who formerly worked primarily for corporate bosses and crime lords.

Favours Gone Wrong

The work load of Joris' role load led him to dangerous recourses as a stims supplier. One bad batch harmed a corporate head and put Joris' safety on the line, forcing him on the run.

Discovery For The People

Joris wants a fresh start where he can shake his corporate pursuit and focus on helping the common people. He hopes to discover something that can advance his field of practice.
