Gruyu

An engineer who quit his job to explore the galaxy.

Grucifferous "Gruyu" Yurovich [He/Him] is played by Petar.

Pushed To Adventure

Gruyu was always pushed by society to follow a corporate path that others set out for him, fixing other peoples' machines. One day, he snapped: he bought a ticket to pursue his dreams and explore the Universe.

Junkyard Tinker

Gruyu has a talent for finding things that others might consider scrap and building cool and useful things from them.

Free Spirit

Jaded from his corporate years, Gruyu learned to prioritise his freedom and enjoy the experiences and community he can find, although getting one back on the corporates wouldn't be bad either!
